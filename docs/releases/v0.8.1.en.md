A quick follow-up to last release's auto-updates — a couple of visual bugs and a new setting.

## New

- 🔔 **"Automatically check for updates" toggle** in Settings → General (on by default). Turn it off to skip the silent background check shortly after launch — the manual "Check for updates" button in Settings → About always works either way.

## Fixes

- 🎯 Toggle switches in Settings (autostart, minimize to tray, Steam Cloud warning, auto-check updates) had their knob sitting slightly off-center, nudged down and to the side — now perfectly centered.
- 🔄 The "Check for updates" button used to vanish entirely while a check was running — it now stays put and just disables itself until the check finishes.
