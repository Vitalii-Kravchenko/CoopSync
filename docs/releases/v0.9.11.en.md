Six fixes from the backlog — tray toasts, restore feedback, and a race with the background autopush.

## Fixes

- 🔔 **Update toast now actually opens the app** — clicking the "update available" Windows notification did nothing while CoopSync was sitting hidden in the tray. It's now handled directly by the app itself instead of a window that might not be listening yet.
- 🔕 **No more duplicate update entries** — quitting and relaunching from the tray while the same version was still the latest added a fresh bell entry every time. It's remembered properly now.
- ⏳ **Restore shows it's actually working** — restoring an older save version used to just grey out the buttons with no other sign of progress. There's a spinner now.
- ⏳ **Avatar crop waits for the real upload** — hitting Apply on a cropped avatar closed the window immediately, before the upload had actually finished. It now stays open with a spinner and confirms with a banner once it's really done.
- ✖️ **Cleaner quick toasts** — removed the pointless close button on the small sync notifications; they already vanish on their own after 5 seconds.
- 🔒 **Restore can't collide with the background autopush** — right after you exit a game, CoopSync quietly pushes your save in the background. Restore is now disabled for that game until that push actually lands, so the two can't fight over the same data.
