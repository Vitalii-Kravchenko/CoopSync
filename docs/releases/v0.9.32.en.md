A manual refresh button for your games list, and a clear confirmation when you remove a custom game.

## New

- 🔄 **A refresh button next to "Installed games"** — rechecks your whole games list and its sync statuses on the spot. Before this, the only way to force a recheck was leaving the Games tab and coming back.
- 🗑️ **A banner confirms removing a custom game** — success or failure, instead of just silently returning to the list (or, on failure, only showing an error tucked inside the confirmation dialog).
