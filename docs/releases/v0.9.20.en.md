A small timing bug on the Friends tab.

## Fixes

- 👥 **A friend no longer briefly shows up twice right after accepting** — pending invites and accepted members come from two separate GitHub calls, not one snapshot. For about a second right after someone accepted, those two lists could disagree — invites not yet reflecting the accept while members already did — showing the same friend as both "waiting to accept" and an actual member at once. Both are now fetched together, and membership always wins if the two ever disagree again.
