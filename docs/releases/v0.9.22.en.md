Custom games get a real cover now, not just a text placeholder.

## New

- 🖼️ **Add or change a custom game's cover art** — a manually-added game had no Steam poster to fall back on, just a plain text tile. Now you can pick your own cover and crop it to the right shape, right when adding the game (next to "Add .exe manually") or later from its detail screen — the same pick-and-crop flow the profile avatar already uses, just a 2:3 poster instead of a circle.
