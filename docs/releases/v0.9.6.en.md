A notification bell, so friend activity and app news don't just flash by and disappear.

## New

- 🔔 **Notification bell** — a persistent history of things worth knowing about: a new app version, new supported games, a friend accepting or declining your invite, or losing access to a friend's shared storage. Lives next to Support in the titlebar; items mark themselves read as you actually scroll past them, not just for opening the panel.
- 🖥️ **Windows notification for a friend's save** — a toast appears when a friend uploads a new save while you weren't looking, even with CoopSync sitting quietly in the tray.
- 📌 **"History" tab badge** — lights up when a friend's save you haven't seen yet lands in the shared storage.
