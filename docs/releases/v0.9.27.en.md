The real fix for a custom game's cover not reaching your co-op partner — plus a layout tweak.

## Fixes

- 🤝 **A custom game's cover (or a brand-new custom game) now actually shows up for your co-op partner without them lifting a finger** — a background check already picked these up correctly every couple of minutes, but only ever told the app to refresh the screen when there was also a new save version to report. A cover-only change quietly sat correct with nothing to show for it until you switched tabs or restarted. Now it always refreshes.
- 🔼 **"Remove game" moved to the top of a custom game's details** — next to its cover and name, instead of below its entire sync history, which meant scrolling all the way down on a game with a long history just to find it.
