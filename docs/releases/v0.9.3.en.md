A tiny one: the "update available" notification used to take its time.

## Fixes

- ⚡ The silent startup check for updates waited a hardcoded 10 seconds after launch before even starting — on top of the actual network round-trip to GitHub, that meant 15-20 seconds before you'd see the "new version available" notice. Cut the wait down to 2 seconds; the window is already up and there's nothing else competing for the network at that point anyway.
