Three fixes found while actually trying out last version's "adopt as your own storage" feature.

## Fixes

- 🔳 **The "save your copy?" dialog's buttons now fit on one line** — depending on language and window size, the three buttons could wrap onto two lines. Widened the dialog so they fit properly.
- 🔁 **Adopting local history no longer refuses if you already have your own storage** — it used to hard-block with "sort that out first" if the account already had a saves repo from an earlier host stint. Now it just updates that existing storage with the local history instead.
- 🔄 **Settings now shows the new storage right away after adopting** — it used to keep displaying the old host's info until something else happened to trigger a refresh, even though the adoption itself had already succeeded.
