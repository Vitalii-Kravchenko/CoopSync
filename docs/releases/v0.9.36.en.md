Rename your custom games, fewer clicks to configure a save path, and a notification you were missing.

## New

- ✏️ **Rename a custom game** — change its name from the game's details screen, not just the cover. The rename syncs to your co-op partner too, and the shared save folder and version history move with it instead of getting orphaned. Names can no longer contain characters like `:` or `/` — those silently break sync on Windows (the same class of bug that used to stop custom-game covers from reaching a partner), so they're now rejected up front with a clear message instead.
- 📁 **Picking a save folder saves it immediately** — no more separate "Save" click after Browse.
- 💡 **"Needs setup" now points at exactly what's missing** — opening a game that still needs its save folder highlights that field instead of leaving you to guess.
- 🔄 **The Games tab refreshes itself after any manual edit** — save path, excluded files, .exe, cover, or name — instead of showing a stale size/status until you switch tabs or hit refresh.

## Fixes

- 🔔 **A friend removing a game now actually notifies you** — it used to just vanish from your list with nothing in the bell.
- 🧩 **The exclude-files card's title and refresh button no longer overflow their box** on narrower widths.
