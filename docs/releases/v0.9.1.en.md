A quick follow-up fixing update-checking itself.

## Fixes

- 🔧 Downloading an update could fail with a 404 — the installer's filename and the one the updater looked for didn't quite match. Fixed for good going forward.
- 🎯 The "Check for updates" button in Settings could get squished into wrapped, unreadable text when the status message next to it was long.
- 🔁 The silent background update check only ran once, shortly after launch — since CoopSync stays running in the tray for days at a time, it now also re-checks periodically instead of only at startup.
