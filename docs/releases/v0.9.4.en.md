The Friends tab grew from a bare list into a proper group overview.

## New

- 🎨 **Friends tab redesign** — the shared storage now has its own summary strip (repository name, member and game counters — click to open it on GitHub), and members turned into cards with real sync activity: when each player synced last and how often, taken from the same push history the History tab shows. The games counter only counts games that were actually uploaded to storage at least once. And no fake "online" dots — CoopSync has no presence tracking, so it shows honest activity instead.
- ↩️ **Cancel a pending invite** — sent an invitation to the wrong username or changed your mind? The owner can now take it back right from the invite card, as long as it hasn't been accepted yet.
