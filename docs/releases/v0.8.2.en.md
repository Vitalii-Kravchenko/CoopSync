Makes the auto-updater actually noticeable, plus a couple of small fixes.

## New

- 🔔 **Update banner on the Games tab** — same style as the Steam Cloud warning, with its own Download / Restart button. Previously an available update only showed up in Settings, easy to miss.

## Fixes

- 🎯 The sync-status badge on game cards (e.g. "Synced") had its dot sitting slightly off the text's optical center — tightened the line spacing so it lines up properly.
- 🪲 "Couldn't check for updates" in Settings now shows the actual underlying error alongside the message, instead of always the same generic line regardless of what actually failed.
