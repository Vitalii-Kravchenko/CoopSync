The actual reason a deleted custom game could come back on its own — this time it was the app window, not the sync itself.

## Fixes

- 🗑️ **A deleted custom game no longer reappears in the list on its own.** The previous release fixed a git-level race, but a separate bug was still there underneath: the games screen fires off several independent background refreshes (on a timer, on returning to the tab, after a manual sync, after deleting a game...), and they could resolve out of order — an older check that happened to still be running would sometimes finish AFTER a newer one and silently paint over the correct, just-updated list with its stale data, deleted game included. Now only the most recently started check is ever allowed to update what's on screen.
