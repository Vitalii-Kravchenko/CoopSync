Losing access to a shared storage — kicked, or leaving on purpose — doesn't have to mean losing everything.

## New

- 🏠 **Turn a shared repo into your own** — leaving a shared storage now offers a choice instead of just wiping everything: keep your local copy as your own, self-owned storage, with the *full* version history (not just the current save), or just leave like before. Friends would need a fresh invite to the new one.

## Fixes

- 📜 **History survives losing access** — viewing a game's sync history used to require a live connection to the shared repo every single time; if you'd been kicked, left, or were just offline, the whole screen errored out — even though the history was still sitting on your PC the whole time. It now falls back to what's already there. Restoring a version still needs live access, so it keeps showing its own clear error if you try that while access is actually gone.
