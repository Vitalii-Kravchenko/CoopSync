Custom game covers now update every time, not just the first time — plus a deleted game staying deleted, and friends' profile pictures staying fresh.

## Fixes

- 🖼️ **Changing a custom game's cover now actually reaches your partner every time, not just the first time.** The very first cover a partner ever saw for a game synced fine, but any later change to it never did — a one-time-only guard meant to protect an "intentional local choice" was quietly blocking every update after that, forever. There's no such thing as a local-only cover choice for a shared game, so it now always mirrors the latest one.
- 🔁 **Fixed a deleted custom game sometimes reappearing on its own after a while.** A direct add/remove/cover action and the ~2min background check could both be mid-write to the shared history at the same time and step on each other — most likely what caused a just-deleted game to get "resurrected" by a background check that read a not-yet-updated registry. These now always run one at a time.
- 🙂 **A friend's profile picture no longer gets stuck on however it looked when the app started.** It was cached for the rest of the session by design — someone changing their picture while you were both already in the app just never showed up until a restart. Now it re-checks along with everything else that already refreshes in the background.
