A reliability patch — one more fix for a startup crash on certain AMD cards.

## Fixes

- 🖥️ **The app could still fail to open on some AMD cards** (e.g. RX 6600) even after the v0.6.1 fix — Chromium was still spawning a separate GPU process even with hardware acceleration disabled, and that process was the one crashing ("GPU process isn't usable. Goodbye."). The GPU service now runs inside the app's main process instead, so there's no separate process left to crash.
