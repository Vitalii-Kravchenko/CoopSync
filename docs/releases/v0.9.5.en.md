History finally shows who did what, and stops dumping the whole list on you at once.

## New

- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **Player avatars in history** — both the History tab and each game's own history now show a Player column with the person's avatar instead of a plain "Uploaded · login" pill.
- 🔍 **Filter history by game or player** — a compact search box above the table narrows things down as you type.
- 📄 **"Show more" instead of one long table** — history now loads just enough rows to fit your window, a button reveals more on demand, and a message tells you clearly when you've reached the end.
