Cleans up who's allowed to touch the shared storage.

## New

- 🚪 **"Leave shared storage"** for anyone who joined a friend's storage — drops you back to picking a role (host your own or join someone else) without touching your local saves.
- 👋 **Remove a collaborator** — the storage owner can now kick someone from the Friends tab, not just invite.

## Fixes

- 🔒 "Delete repository" in Settings was visible to everyone, including a friend who only joined — clicking it as a non-owner didn't actually touch the host's storage (it silently tried to delete a repo under your own account instead), but still reset your local sync state as if it had. Now owner-only; everyone else sees "Leave shared storage" there instead.
- 🔒 The same missing check let a joined member see the "Invite a friend" box too. Owner-only now, and enforced on the backend as well as in the UI.
