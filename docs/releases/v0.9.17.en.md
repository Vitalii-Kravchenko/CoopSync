There's no server here — the app only knows what's changed when it asks GitHub again. Two spots where it wasn't asking often enough.

## Fixes

- 🔄 **Friends tab notices an accepted invite on its own** — inviting someone used to leave "waiting for them to accept" stuck on screen even after they'd actually accepted, until you switched to another tab and back. Now it polls every 5s while there's a pending invite and you're actually looking at the tab, and stops on its own once nobody's left pending.
- 🔄 **Settings notices when a host removes you** — unlike the other tabs, Settings never rechecked the repo/host status after its first load, so a removed collaborator kept seeing "connected to X's storage" indefinitely. It now rereads on returning to the tab, and also the moment the background access-revoked notification arrives. That also uncovered a second bug: once the status correctly showed "no access," the empty state offered a "Create storage" button meant for hosts — clicking it would've silently created an orphaned repo under your own account while still pointed at the old host. Replaced with a clear message and a button into the existing leave/keep-your-own-copy flow.
