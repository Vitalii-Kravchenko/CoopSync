Full sync history, and a way back if a save goes wrong.

## New

- 📜 **Full sync history with pages** — history no longer trims itself to the last 50 pushes; every sync is kept, browsable through numbered pages instead of a "Show more" button.
- ⏪ **Restore an older save version** — pick any past version in a game's own history and revert to it. It becomes a new entry at the top of history rather than a branch, so a friend with shared access picks it up automatically the next time they launch the game, exactly like any other sync.

## Fixes

- 🎨 The app logo in the titlebar could flicker and change color right when opening Settings for the first time in a session — two logo instances on screen at once shared the same internal SVG ids.
- 📐 A game's own sync history screen could force an unwanted scrollbar, with the page bar pushed just past the bottom edge — its header is taller than the general History tab's, and the row-fitting math didn't account for the difference.
