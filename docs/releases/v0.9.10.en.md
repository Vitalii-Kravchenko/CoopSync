An actual crop tool for your profile picture, and a couple of small polish fixes.

## New

- ✂️ **Crop your profile picture** — picking a new avatar now opens a crop step: drag to reposition, use the slider to zoom, and frame exactly the part of the photo you want before it's saved as your (circular) avatar. No more guessing how a tall or wide photo gets auto-cropped.

## Fixes

- 🔒 A confirmation dialog (e.g. restoring an older save version) could still be closed by clicking outside it while the action was in progress — reopening it let you fire a second, overlapping request. Clicking outside is now blocked the same way the buttons already were.
- 🔔 A notification bell entry for an available update stayed unread forever if you updated without opening the bell panel first. It's now automatically marked read once you're actually running that version (or newer).
