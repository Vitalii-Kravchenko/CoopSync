Seven fixes found from actually using custom games day to day.

## Fixes

- 🌐 **Installer now speaks your Windows language** — the Smart App Control warning before install used to always show in English, even though it's translated into all 10 languages. It now matches your Windows display language automatically, falling back to English if yours isn't one of the ten.
- 🎯 **A custom game's picked .exe now actually stays picked** — reopening its detail screen showed no selected executable at all, even though it was saved correctly. Just a display bug that made it look lost.
- 🏷️ **"Added manually" now sits next to the game's name** instead of on its own line underneath.
- 🖼️ **File pickers (cover art, avatar) now follow your chosen language** — they used to always show in Ukrainian regardless of what you'd picked in Settings.
- 🙂 **Your own avatar survives a full reinstall** — it used to disappear from everywhere (your view and your friend's) after uninstalling and reinstalling, even though it was still sitting safely on GitHub. Recovered automatically now.
- 🗂️ **Exclude files right when adding a custom game** — no more adding it first, then hunting for the exclude option afterward in its details.
- ☁️ **A cover that fails to reach GitHub now tells you** — adding a custom game or changing its cover could silently fail to sync to your co-op partner. Now you get a warning with a "Try again" button instead of it vanishing unnoticed.
