A reliability patch — the most important part fixes two bugs that could permanently break sync for a co-op partner. Also added: shared avatars.

## New

- 🖼️ **Avatars now sync between you and your friend.** Set a custom picture in Settings and it uploads to your shared storage — your co-op partner will see it too (and you'll see theirs) instead of a generic placeholder.

## Fixes

- 🔧 **Critical: sync could break permanently.** If the app closed or crashed mid-upload, its internal working copy could be left "dirty" — every sync afterward (history, upload, download) would then fail with a "local changes would be overwritten by merge" error, with no way to recover except manual cleanup. The app now resets that internal state automatically before syncing.
- 🔑 **Critical: uploading could fail entirely** with "unable to auto-detect email address" on a machine where git has never been configured with a name/email. The app now sets its own commit identity instead of depending on machine-level git config.
- 👤 Sync history and "last updated by" info now correctly show who actually performed the action — it used to always show the storage owner, even when your co-op partner was the one who synced.
- 👤 In the members list (when you've joined a friend's storage), the "owner" row used to show your own nickname instead of the real owner — now it always shows the real one. The placeholder avatar (when no picture is available yet) is also consistent everywhere now.
- 🔄 The Friends tab no longer shows stale data — it refreshes automatically every time you open it, instead of only once at app startup.
- 🪟 Launching the app a second time (e.g. double-clicking the shortcut while it's already running in the tray) no longer opens a duplicate instance — it just brings the existing window to front.
- 🧭 Fixed the "no storage access" warning on the connect screen being misaligned to the left instead of lining up with the rest of the setup steps.
